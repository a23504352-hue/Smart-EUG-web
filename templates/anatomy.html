What you're seeing

  • Uterus body (pear-shaped semi-transparent outer) — the muscle layer that contracts during menstruation
  • Endometrium (red inner layer) — the lining that thickens during follicular/luteal and sheds during your period. Watch it shrink when you click "Menstrual".
  • Fallopian tubes — the egg travels through these from ovary to uterus over ~5 days
  • Ovaries (pink spheres) — produce eggs and hormones. During ovulation, one follicle glows yellow as the egg is released.
  • Cervix + vaginal canal — the path menstrual blood takes out of the body
