What is EUG?

EUG (electrouterography) measures tiny electrical signals from the uterus through 3 abdominal electrodes — like ECG measures the heart. During menstruation, these signals are stronger, more rhythmic, and more synchronized across electrodes. Our AI distinguishes menstrual contractions from non-menstrual activity.

7-step AI pipeline

  1. Load — BIOPAC .txt or .mat or ESP32 stream
  2. Filter — 50 Hz notch + 0.1-3 Hz bandpass
  3. RMS envelope — 10s smoothing window
  4. Adaptive threshold — median + 1.5 × MAD per channel
  5. Consensus — at least 2 of 3 channels must agree
  6. 14 features per contraction
  7. GradientBoosting model + per-phase threshold matching
